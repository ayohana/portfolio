Hello, my name is Adela.



I am a Per Diem Registered Nurse aspiring to become a Full Stack Developer by the end of 2020. I am currently enrolled at Epicodus, a six-month coding bootcamp graduating this July.



I graduated from University of Washington with a B.S. in Biology/Physiology in 2013 and Seattle University with a B.S. in Nursing in 2015. Outside of school/work, I love to spend my time in the kitchen (be it cooking or eating) or watch Korean dramas at home. You may also find me walking my dog, Chloe, out with my husband, Ivan in Issaquah Highlands, Washington.
